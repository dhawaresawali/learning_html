PAREKAR GLOBAL VENTURE PRIVATE LIMITED

PAREKAR GLOBAL VENTURES PRIVATE LIMITED is a private limited company incorporated in India on 7th May 2026 under the Companies Act, 2013. The company is registered with the Registrar of Companies (ROC), nagpur and is currently in activestatus. It is managed by three directors,Piyush Keshavraj Parekar and Pranav Keshavraj Parekar and Sawali Bapu Dhaware. Based on its company classification, Parekar Global Ventures Private Limited is primarily involved in the wholesale trade of electronic and telecommunication equipment and related products. As a newly established company, it aims to expand its business operations while providing quality products and services in the trading sector. The company operates with the objective of contributing to the growth of the electronics and telecommunications industry in India.

Aim

To become a trusted and leading company in the wholesale trading of electronic and telecommunication equipment by providing high-quality products, maintaining strong customer relationships, adopting innovative technologies, and delivering reliable solutions that contribute to the growth of businesses and the electronics industry in India.
