**Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Today's Generation** Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an important part of today's generation. It helps people complete tasks faster and more efficiently. AI is used in smartphones, online shopping, healthcare, education, banking, and transportation. It can learn, solve problems, and make decisions based on data. While AI makes life easier and improves productivity, it should be used responsibly to protect privacy and ensure ethical use. As technology continues to grow, AI will play an even bigger role in our daily lives and future.
